# 2026-07-12 Mechanical Block Lock Prototypes

Small fit-test coupons for the smart mechanical block concept. Each numbered
folder contains separate printable STL parts for one lock principle.

The parts are intentionally small to keep print time and material use low. They
are first-pass PETG prototypes with nominal clearances around 0.35-0.45 mm.
Print one prototype folder at a time, test the fit, then tune the SCAD source if
your printer/filament needs a tighter or looser fit.

## Prototype Index

- `prototype 1` - dovetail slide: male rail and socket block.
- `prototype 2` - quarter-turn bayonet plug and socket.
- `prototype 3` - flexible snap hook and catch socket.
- `prototype 4` - two receiver blocks plus a wedge key.
- `prototype 5` - T-slider and T-track.
- `prototype 6` - split-pin male block and matching socket.
- `prototype 7` - M10 printed thread plug and socket.
- `prototype 8` - sliding detent rail with printed bump and indexed track.
- `prototype 9` - cam clamp body, cam lever, and pin.
- `prototype 10` - rack bar and printed pawl latch.
